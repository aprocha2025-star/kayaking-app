About this event

Join TD employees, Women in Tech interns, and friends for a morning of kayaking, conversation, and community on Toronto's waterfront.

We'll launch from Cherry Beach for a guided kayak journey with Lawrence Warriner, exploring Toronto's waterfront ecology, wildlife, and hidden natural beauty from the water — heading toward Biidaasigie Park or the Toronto Islands. Along the way, please feel free to collect any garbage we see floating in the water to help keep our shared waterways clean. All tools provided.

Kayaking experience

Beginner-friendly. Participants will paddle in a relaxed group setting with plenty of opportunities for conversation throughout the journey. Kayaks and safety equipment are provided. A certified lifeguard will be paddling with the group.

What to bring
👕 Comfortable clothing suitable for paddling
💧 Water bottle
🧴 Sunscreen, bug spray and hat
🧺 Small towel if desired
👟 Comfortable footwear that can get wet
